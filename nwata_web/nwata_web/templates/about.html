Our Story

How Nwata came to be and why we're passionate about changing the future of work

Nwata was born from a simple observation: in today's digital workplace, employees generate vast amounts of valuable data about their work patterns, skills, and productivity, but they have no ownership or control over this information.

Meanwhile, employers struggle to get meaningful insights into team performance without resorting to surveillance tools that erode trust and violate privacy. The current solutions create a lose-lose situation for everyone involved.

We believe there's a better way. A way where employees own their work data, stored securely in personal encrypted wallets. A way where employers get actionable insights through transparent, consent-driven sharing. A way where productivity tracking becomes a tool for empowerment rather than surveillance.

This vision drives everything we do at Nwata. We're not just building software—we're building a new paradigm for work identity, one that respects individual privacy while enabling better collaboration and growth for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Nwata's approach and technology

How is employee data protected?

All data is encrypted locally on the employee's device using industry-standard encryption. Data is only shared when the employee explicitly consents and can be revoked at any time. We never have access to unencrypted data.

Can employers see raw activity data?

No. Employers only see aggregated, consented summaries. They never have access to raw activity logs, individual app usage, or personal data. The employee controls exactly what is shared and for how long.

How does auditing work?

Auditing is consent-driven. Employees can choose to share specific time periods or project data for compliance purposes. All sharing is logged and can be audited by the employee, ensuring complete transparency.

Is Nwata GDPR/POPIA compliant?

Yes. Our privacy-first design and consent-driven approach align with GDPR, POPIA, and other privacy regulations. Employees have full control over their data and can export or delete it at any time.
